How to Test the Extension:

  1. Install the Extension: Go to chrome://extensions/, enable Developer mode, click "Load unpacked", select the extension folder
  2. Open Extension: Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar
  3. Upload Face (Optional): Click "Upload Your Face" and select a clear photo of yourself
  4. Configure Settings: Choose your preferred AI style and intensity
  5. Run AI Overlay: Click "Run AI Overlay" button
  6. Watch the Magic: See the product images transform with AI-generated personalized versions!
  7. Reset: Click "Reset Images" to restore originals
